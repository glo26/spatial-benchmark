# SpatialOps: 5 Key Metrics with Mathematical Models

## 1. Task Completion Rate (TCR)
- **Description**: Percentage of tasks successfully completed by the AI agent.
- **Formula**: $$ TCR = \frac{\text{Tasks Completed}}{\text{Total Tasks}} \times 100\% $$

## 2. Human-AI Latency Ratio (HLR)
- **Description**: Ratio of time taken by a human professional vs. an AI agent to complete the same task.
- **Formula**: $$ HLR = \frac{\text{Time}_{\text{human}}}{\text{Time}_{\text{AI}}} $$

## 3. Operational Cost Savings (OCS)
- **Description**: Estimated dollar savings from using AI agents for spatial planning tasks.
- **Formula**: $$ OCS = (\text{Time}_{\text{human}} - \text{Time}_{\text{AI}}) \times \text{Hourly Rate}_{\text{human}} - \text{Cost}_{\text{AI}} $$

## 4. Efficacy Score (ES)
- **Description**: Composite score combining accuracy, reasoning quality, and efficiency.
- **Formula**: $$ ES = w_1 \times \text{Accuracy} + w_2 \times \text{Reasoning Quality} + w_3 \times \text{Efficiency} $$
  - Where $w_1 + w_2 + w_3 = 1$

## 5. Scalability Index (SI)
- **Description**: Measure of the AI agent's ability to handle increasing task complexity and volume.
- **Formula**: $$ SI = \frac{\text{Tasks Completed}_{\text{high complexity}}}{\text{Tasks Completed}_{\text{low complexity}}} \times \frac{\text{Time}_{\text{low complexity}}}{\text{Time}_{\text{high complexity}}} $$
